# Dashboard Concept

## Objective

This document outlines a simplified governance dashboard concept for the AIMS-Guard prototype.

The objective is to explore how organisations may eventually visualise governance visibility, oversight activity and operational AI governance status within a lightweight governance environment.

---

## Example Dashboard Areas

| Dashboard Area | Governance Purpose |
|---|---|
| AI Systems Under Review | Governance visibility |
| High-Risk AI Systems | Risk monitoring |
| Human Review Queue | Oversight tracking |
| Governance Escalations | Escalation visibility |
| AI Incident Activity | Incident monitoring |
| Governance Readiness Status | Operational governance maturity |

---

## Operational Governance Value

A lightweight governance dashboard may eventually help organisations:

- improve governance visibility
- monitor operational oversight activity
- identify governance bottlenecks
- strengthen accountability tracking
- support implementation-oriented governance monitoring

---

## Future Exploration

Future prototype exploration may include lightweight dashboard simulations, governance workflow visualisation and operational oversight monitoring concepts inspired by ISO/IEC 42001 governance principles.
