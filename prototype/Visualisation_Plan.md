# Visualisation Plan

## Objective

The next phase of the AIMS-Guard prototype will explore lightweight visual representations of operational AI governance activities.

The purpose is to gradually transition from static governance documentation toward visual governance monitoring and oversight-oriented operational visibility concepts.

---

## Planned Visual Exploration Areas

- governance dashboard mockups
- escalation workflow visualisation
- AI system monitoring concepts
- human oversight review interfaces
- governance readiness indicators
- incident tracking visualisation

---

## Governance Goal

The objective is not to build enterprise software infrastructure, but to explore how operational governance concepts may eventually become easier to monitor, review and understand within organisational environments.
