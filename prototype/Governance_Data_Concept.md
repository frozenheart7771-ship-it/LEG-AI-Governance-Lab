# Governance Data Concept

## Objective

This document outlines example governance information that may eventually support operational governance visibility within the AIMS-Guard prototype.

The purpose is to explore how governance-related activities may eventually be organised within lightweight governance monitoring environments.

---

## Example Governance Data Areas

| Governance Area | Example Information |
|---|---|
| AI Systems | system name, department, governance status |
| Human Reviews | reviewer, review status, review date |
| Governance Escalations | escalation reason, severity level, resolution status |
| AI Incidents | incident type, operational impact, action taken |
| Governance Readiness | maturity status, review activity, oversight coverage |

---

## Governance Objective

The objective is to improve governance visibility, operational oversight awareness and implementation-oriented governance monitoring readiness.

---

## Operational Governance Note

The prototype is focused on governance operations and oversight visibility rather than AI model engineering or infrastructure management.
