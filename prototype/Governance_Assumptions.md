# Governance Assumptions

## Purpose

This document outlines example operational assumptions used within the AIMS-Guard governance prototype.

These assumptions are intended to simulate practical governance conditions that organisations may eventually face while deploying AI-enabled systems.

---

## Example Governance Assumptions

- AI systems may generate incorrect or incomplete outputs.
- Human oversight may still be required for high-impact decisions.
- Governance review frequency may vary depending on risk level.
- Certain AI outputs may require escalation before operational use.
- AI-related incidents may require documentation and review.
- Different departments may operate AI systems with varying governance maturity levels.

---

## Operational Governance Objective

The purpose of documenting governance assumptions is to improve governance clarity, oversight visibility and implementation consistency across AI-assisted operational workflows.
