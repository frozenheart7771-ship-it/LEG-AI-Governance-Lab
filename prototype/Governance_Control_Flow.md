# Governance Control Flow

## Objective

This document outlines a simplified operational governance flow for AI system oversight within the AIMS-Guard prototype.

---

## Example Governance Flow

AI System Proposed
        ↓
AI Risk Review Initiated
        ↓
Human Oversight Requirement Checked
        ↓
Risk Level Assessed
        ↓
Approval or Escalation Decision
        ↓
AI System Deployment Review
        ↓
Periodic Governance Monitoring

---

## Governance Purpose

The objective of this workflow is to demonstrate how operational AI governance checkpoints may eventually help organisations:

- improve governance visibility
- strengthen accountability
- introduce structured oversight
- reduce unmanaged AI risks
- support governance readiness
