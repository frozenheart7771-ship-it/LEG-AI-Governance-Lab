# Scenario: High-Risk AI Review

## Example Scenario

An organisation introduces an AI-assisted screening tool intended to support candidate shortlisting within recruitment workflows.

During initial governance review, concerns are raised regarding:
- transparency of decision logic
- potential fairness risks
- limited visibility into training data sources
- insufficient human review controls

---

## Governance Response

The governance review process recommends:

- mandatory human review before operational use
- restricted pilot deployment
- additional fairness assessment
- governance monitoring during initial rollout
- escalation pathway for unresolved concerns

---

## Governance Observation

This scenario demonstrates how operational governance review processes may help organisations introduce structured oversight before deploying potentially high-impact AI systems into operational environments.
