# Governance Escalation Workflow

## Example Escalation Process

AI Risk or Incident Identified
        ↓
Initial Operational Review
        ↓
Risk Severity Assessment
        ↓
Human Oversight Evaluation
        ↓
Escalation to Governance Review
        ↓
Decision: Approve / Restrict / Pause
        ↓
Governance Monitoring & Documentation

---

## Example Escalation Conditions

- fairness concern detected
- sensitive data exposure identified
- AI-generated high-risk recommendation
- repeated incorrect outputs
- unresolved governance review findings

---

## Governance Purpose

This workflow demonstrates how organisations may eventually introduce structured escalation pathways for AI-related governance concerns, operational risks and oversight reviews.
