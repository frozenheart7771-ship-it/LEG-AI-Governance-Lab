# Human Review Triggers

## Objective

This document outlines example situations where AI-generated outputs may require mandatory human review before approval or operational use.

---

## Example Human Review Triggers

| Trigger Scenario | Human Review Required |
|---|---|
| High-risk AI decision affecting customers | Yes |
| AI-generated legal or compliance content | Yes |
| AI output containing sensitive personal data | Yes |
| Bias or fairness concern identified | Yes |
| Confidence score below defined threshold | Yes |
| AI-generated medical or financial recommendation | Yes |

---

## Governance Purpose

The objective of these review triggers is to support operational oversight, reduce unmanaged AI risks and strengthen accountability within AI-assisted workflows.
