# Simulator Training Concept

## Objective

The AIMS-Guard initiative is being explored as a lightweight operational AI governance readiness simulator intended to support governance awareness, oversight education and operational governance preparedness.

The objective is not formal certification or regulatory approval.

The simulator is intended to help organisations, operational teams and governance stakeholders explore governance scenarios, oversight workflows and accountability concepts within simplified governance-oriented simulation environments.

---

## Example Simulator Activities

- governance readiness simulations
- mock governance reviews
- human oversight exercises
- escalation workflow simulations
- incident response walkthroughs
- accountability trigger scenarios

---

## Example Educational Outcomes

The simulator may eventually help stakeholders:

- improve governance awareness
- understand oversight responsibilities
- identify governance visibility gaps
- explore escalation decision-making
- strengthen operational governance thinking

---

## Governance Philosophy

The simulator is focused on practical operational governance understanding rather than theoretical compliance-only learning.

The objective is to make governance concepts more visible, interactive and operationally understandable for organisational stakeholders.
