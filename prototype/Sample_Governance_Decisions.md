# Sample Governance Decisions

| Scenario | Governance Decision | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| AI screening tool shows possible fairness concern | Deployment paused | Human review required before operational use |
| Internal AI assistant generates unverified policy content | Manual validation initiated | Governance accuracy review required |
| Customer support AI repeatedly generates incorrect responses | Escalated to governance review | Operational reliability concern identified |
| AI tool introduced without governance registration | Governance onboarding required | Visibility and accountability gap detected |

---

## Governance Purpose

These examples demonstrate how operational governance decisions may eventually support oversight, accountability and responsible AI deployment management within organisations.
