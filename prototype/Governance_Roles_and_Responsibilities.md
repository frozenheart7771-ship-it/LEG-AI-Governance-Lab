# Governance Roles and Responsibilities

## Objective

This document outlines example operational governance responsibilities that may exist within AI-enabled organisational environments.

The purpose is to demonstrate how accountability and oversight responsibilities may eventually be distributed across governance, operational and business functions.

---

## Example Governance Roles

| Role | Example Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Governance Lead | Oversees governance review activities |
| Operations Manager | Monitors operational AI usage |
| Human Reviewer | Validates high-risk AI outputs |
| Compliance Reviewer | Reviews governance and regulatory concerns |
| Department Owner | Maintains visibility into departmental AI systems |
| Incident Coordinator | Tracks AI-related incidents and escalations |

---

## Governance Objective

Clearly defined governance responsibilities may help organisations:

- improve accountability clarity
- strengthen governance ownership
- support operational oversight
- reduce governance ambiguity
- improve governance readiness maturity

---

## Operational Governance Note

In many organisations, governance responsibilities may evolve gradually as AI adoption expands across operational teams and business workflows.
