# Prototype Architecture Overview

## Objective

The AIMS-Guard prototype is being explored as a lightweight operational AI governance environment focused on governance visibility, accountability tracking and oversight-oriented workflow simulation.

---

## Current Prototype Components

| Component | Governance Purpose |
|---|---|
| Governance Control Flow | Simulates governance review stages |
| Human Review Triggers | Identifies situations requiring oversight |
| Incident Governance Log | Tracks operational AI incidents |
| Governance Escalation Workflow | Simulates escalation pathways |
| AI System Status Register | Maintains governance visibility |
| Governance Assumptions | Documents operational governance conditions |
| Sample Governance Decisions | Demonstrates oversight decision examples |

---

## Future Exploration Areas

- lightweight governance dashboard
- governance workflow simulation
- operational risk indicators
- governance status monitoring
- oversight activity tracking
- implementation-oriented governance controls

---

## Governance Vision

The long-term objective is to explore how operational AI governance concepts may eventually evolve from static documentation into lightweight governance-support environments inspired by ISO/IEC 42001 governance thinking.
