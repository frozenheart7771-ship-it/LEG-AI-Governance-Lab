# Governance Readiness Assessment

## Purpose

This assessment is intended to help organisations evaluate their current level of operational preparedness for responsible AI governance, oversight and accountability activities.

The assessment focuses on practical governance readiness rather than technical AI capability maturity.

# Governance Maturity Notes

| Maturity Level | General Characteristics |
|---|---|
| Early Stage | Limited governance visibility and informal oversight |
| Developing | Initial governance controls and review activities established |
| Operational | Structured oversight processes and accountability mechanisms implemented |
| Mature | Governance integrated into operational decision-making and review cycles |
