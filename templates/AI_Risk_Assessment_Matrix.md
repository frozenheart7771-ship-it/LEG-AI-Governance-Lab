# AI Risk Assessment Matrix

## Purpose

This matrix is intended to support structured identification and assessment of operational, ethical, compliance and governance risks associated with AI systems and AI-enabled processes.

The template is designed for practical governance reviews and implementation-oriented oversight activities.

---

# Risk Assessment Matrix

| Risk ID | Risk Description | Potential Impact | Likelihood | Human Oversight Required | Mitigation Approach | Risk Owner | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| R-001 | Inaccurate AI-generated outputs | Operational decision errors | Medium | Yes | Human review before action | Operations Lead | Open |
| R-002 | Unauthorised AI tool usage | Data exposure or governance gaps | High | Yes | Internal AI usage controls | Governance Team | Open |
| R-003 | Lack of explainability | Reduced accountability | Medium | Yes | Documentation and review process | AI Owner | Monitoring |
| R-004 | Bias in AI-assisted decisions | Ethical and reputational concerns | High | Yes | Bias testing and escalation process | Compliance Lead | Open |
