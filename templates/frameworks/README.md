# Governance Frameworks

This section contains governance concepts, operational accountability models and implementation-oriented oversight approaches developed as part of the LEG AI Governance Lab initiative.

The objective is to explore practical governance structures that organisations may use while deploying AI-enabled systems and workflows responsibly.
