# Governance Dashboard Screen Concept

## Objective

This concept explores how operational AI governance activity may eventually be visualised through a lightweight governance dashboard environment within the AIMS-Guard initiative.

The objective is to simplify governance visibility for operational teams, governance reviewers and organisational leadership.

---

# Example Dashboard Sections

## Governance Overview Panel
- Total AI systems monitored
- High-risk systems identified
- Pending governance reviews
- Active governance incidents
- Human oversight alerts

---

## Governance Status Indicators
- Governance readiness score
- Escalation status visibility
- Oversight review activity
- Documentation completion level
- Incident response readiness

---

## Human Oversight Queue
- Systems awaiting human review
- Escalated governance cases
- Pending intervention decisions
- Governance accountability owners

---

## Governance Activity Feed
- Recent governance escalations
- Incident review activity
- Governance review updates
- Oversight actions completed

---

# Governance Objective

The objective is not operational surveillance.

The purpose is to improve governance transparency, accountability visibility and operational governance coordination for AI-supported organisational activities.
