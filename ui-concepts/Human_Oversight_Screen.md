# Human Oversight Screen Concept

## Objective

This concept explores how governance teams may eventually visualise AI-related cases requiring additional human oversight, escalation review or operational governance attention.

The objective is to improve visibility for governance-sensitive AI activities before operational approval or escalation closure.

---

# Example Oversight Screen Sections

## Oversight Review Queue
- Pending human reviews
- Escalated governance cases
- High-risk AI activities
- Sensitive decision reviews

---

## Escalation Visibility Panel
- Governance escalation status
- Incident severity visibility
- Review priority indicators
- Accountability ownership tracking

---

## Governance Decision Activity
- Human intervention records
- Governance review outcomes
- Escalation closure updates
- Governance observations logged

---

## Governance Alerts
- Missing governance evidence
- Incomplete review workflows
- Delayed oversight response
- Repeated escalation patterns

---

# Governance Objective

The objective is to support governance transparency, operational accountability and human oversight visibility within AI-supported organisational workflows.

The purpose is governance coordination rather than automated decision replacement.
