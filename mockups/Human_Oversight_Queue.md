# Human Oversight Review Queue

## Purpose

This mockup explores how governance teams may eventually monitor AI cases requiring additional human review, escalation or operational oversight.

The objective is to improve governance visibility for potentially sensitive, high-risk or uncertain AI-supported activities.

---

# Example Human Review Queue

| Case ID | AI System | Trigger Reason | Review Status | Assigned Reviewer |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| HR-104 | Recruitment Screening Tool | Potential bias concern | Pending | Governance Lead |
| HR-118 | Financial Recommendation Assistant | High-risk financial guidance | Under Review | Compliance Officer |
| HR-126 | Customer Support AI | Escalated customer complaint | Open | Operations Manager |
| HR-131 | Healthcare Symptom Assistant | Sensitive health-related recommendation | Escalated | Human Oversight Panel |

---

# Example Governance Triggers

- Sensitive decision impact
- Regulatory exposure
- Ethical concern raised
- User complaint escalation
- High-risk classification
- Incomplete governance evidence
- Human override request

---

# Governance Objective

The objective is to ensure that AI-supported decisions with elevated governance sensitivity remain visible to accountable human reviewers before operational approval or escalation closure.
