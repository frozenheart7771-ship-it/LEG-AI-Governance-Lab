# Governance Dashboard Notes

## Design Direction

The dashboard should feel:
- operational
- clean
- governance-oriented
- oversight-focused
- implementation-friendly

The objective is to avoid flashy AI startup aesthetics and instead simulate a lightweight governance monitoring environment.

---

## Possible Dashboard Sections

- AI systems under governance review
- governance escalation alerts
- human review queue
- governance readiness indicators
- incident monitoring activity
- operational governance status

---

## Governance Design Philosophy

The dashboard should support visibility, accountability and operational governance awareness rather than technical AI model management.

The focus is governance operations, not AI engineering infrastructure.
