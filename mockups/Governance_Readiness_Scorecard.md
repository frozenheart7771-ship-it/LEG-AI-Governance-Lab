# Governance Readiness Scorecard

## Purpose

This mockup explores how organisations may eventually visualise operational AI governance readiness across key governance and oversight areas.

The objective is to improve governance visibility before formal audits, certification activities or large-scale AI deployment initiatives.

---

# Example Governance Readiness Areas

| Governance Area | Readiness Status |
|---|---|
| AI System Visibility | Moderate |
| Human Oversight Controls | Developing |
| Incident Escalation Readiness | Moderate |
| Governance Documentation | Developing |
| Accountability Ownership | Strong |
| Governance Review Process | Moderate |
| Shadow AI Visibility | Limited |
| Operational Governance Maturity | Developing |

---

# Example Governance Interpretation

- Strong → governance process reasonably established
- Moderate → governance visibility exists but requires improvement
- Developing → governance controls partially implemented
- Limited → governance process not consistently operational

---

# Governance Objective

The objective is to help organisations identify governance maturity gaps early through simplified operational governance readiness visibility concepts inspired by ISO/IEC 42001 governance thinking.
