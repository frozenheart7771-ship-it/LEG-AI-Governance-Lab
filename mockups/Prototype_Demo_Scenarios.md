# Prototype Demo Scenarios

## Objective

This document outlines example governance situations that may eventually be demonstrated through lightweight operational governance dashboard prototypes within the AIMS-Guard initiative.

---

# Example Demo Scenarios

| Scenario | Governance Activity |
|---|---|
| High-risk recruitment AI identified | Human review escalation triggered |
| Customer complaint against AI recommendation | Incident review initiated |
| AI system introduced without governance registration | Governance visibility alert generated |
| Governance documentation incomplete | Readiness score reduced |
| Multiple unresolved AI incidents detected | Governance escalation status increased |

---

# Governance Demonstration Purpose

The objective of these scenarios is to simulate operational governance visibility, accountability workflows and oversight-oriented governance activity within simplified governance monitoring environments.

---

# Future Prototype Direction

Future prototype exploration may include:
- interactive governance dashboards
- oversight simulation panels
- governance activity monitoring
- operational governance alert concepts
- readiness scoring visibility
