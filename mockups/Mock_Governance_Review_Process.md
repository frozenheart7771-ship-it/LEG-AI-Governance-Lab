# Mock Governance Review Process

## Purpose

This mockup outlines how organisations may eventually conduct lightweight governance readiness reviews before deploying or scaling AI-supported systems.

The objective is to encourage early governance visibility, operational accountability and human oversight preparedness before formal regulatory review or certification activity.

---

# Example Mock Governance Review Stages

| Stage | Review Activity | Governance Objective |
|---|---|---|
| Stage 1 | AI system identification | Confirm visibility of AI-supported processes |
| Stage 2 | Risk classification review | Identify high-risk or sensitive use cases |
| Stage 3 | Human oversight review | Validate escalation and intervention pathways |
| Stage 4 | Governance evidence check | Review documentation completeness |
| Stage 5 | Incident preparedness review | Confirm escalation and response readiness |
| Stage 6 | Leadership governance review | Ensure operational accountability visibility |

---

# Example Governance Outputs

- Governance observations
- Human oversight gaps
- Escalation weaknesses
- Documentation gaps
- Risk visibility concerns
- Operational governance recommendations

---

# Governance Objective

The objective is not formal certification or regulatory approval.

The purpose is to help organisations identify operational governance gaps early through practical governance simulation and structured oversight review exercises.
