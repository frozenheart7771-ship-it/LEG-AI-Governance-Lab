# Executive Governance Dashboard

## Purpose

This mockup explores how executive stakeholders may eventually monitor operational AI governance activities through a simplified governance visibility dashboard.

The concept is focused on practical governance visibility rather than technical AI engineering metrics.

# Governance Design Objective

The objective is to explore how operational AI governance may become easier for leadership teams to monitor through lightweight governance-oriented interfaces inspired by ISO/IEC 42001 governance principles.
